...the last 24 hours


The above map displays all earthquakes that have occurred in the last 24 hours. This map is updated every minute to provide the most recent data. These earthquakes have been symbolized according to their magnitude. Earthquakes with higher magnitudes are shown as larger circles while lower magnitude earthquakes are indicated by smaller circles. Clicking on a specific earthquake will give information about its location and magnitude as well as a link to view further details about that quake.
